Welcome to LiPAD's Shapefile Generator!

To generate a shapefile:

  1. Click and drag the map around to move your area of interest to the view.
  2. Zoom in to your area of interest using the + and - buttons or the mouse scroll wheel.
  3. Click the Polygon button to start drawing a polygon.
  4. Close the polygon by clicking the first point.
  5. Click the download button to generate a zip file containing the shapefile of your area of interest.
  6. Attach the zipped file of your shapefile to your data request e-mail
